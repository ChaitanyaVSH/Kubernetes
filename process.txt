On master node:
 sudo kubeadm init --config kubeadm_conf.yaml


for checking the  images to be downloaded on the master node:
  kubeadm config images pull


on child node:
   sudo "key"(generated by master) (directly after running init.sh)


reset:
sudo kubeadm init --apiserver-advertise-address=IP(IP of the master node) --> this is the alternate way incase you face any issues in "init.sh".


after master has started do the 3 steps:
kubectl apply -f \
https://docs.projectcalico.org/v3.4/getting-started/kubernetes/installation/hosted/calico.yaml


watch pods by using:
watch kubectl get pods --all-namespaces


final verdict:
kubectl describe pod kube-controller-manager-master -n kube-system


create app:
kubectl run javaimage --image=gcr.io/google-samples/echo-java:1.0 --port=8080

kubectl utilization requests might show (maybe kubectl util or kubectl usage are better/shorter)
